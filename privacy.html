MouseEyes is designed with privacy in mind. The application runs locally on your system and does not transmit personal data.

Data Collection

MouseEyes does not collect, store, or transmit personal information. No usage data, analytics, or telemetry are sent to external servers.

Local Operation

All functionality is performed locally on your computer. Mouse position data is used only to animate the on‑screen eyes and is not logged or shared.

Third‑Party Services

MouseEyes does not integrate with third‑party analytics, advertising networks, or tracking services.

Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated as the software evolves. Any changes will be reflected on this page.

If you have questions about privacy or data handling, please contact support via the /support.htmlSupport page.
