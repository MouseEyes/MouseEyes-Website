Never Lose Your Mouse Again

MouseEyes is a lightweight Windows utility that displays animated eyes on your Windows 11 desktop that follow your mouse pointer across multiple monitors — making your cursor instantly visible, even on large or complex setups.

Value Proposition Section

Instantly See Where Your Mouse Is — On Any Screen

MouseEyes adds a playful but powerful visual overlay to your desktop. The animated eyes track your cursor in real time, helping you locate it instantly — even across multiple monitors, high‑resolution displays, or cluttered desktops.

Designed for fun, accessibility, and productivity, MouseEyes runs quietly in the background and stays out of your way until you need it.

Highlight Feature Section

Recover Lost Windows Instantly

MouseEyes includes a unique off‑screen window recovery feature.

If an application window becomes lost outside your visible desktop (common after monitor changes or remote desktop use), MouseEyes can detect it and bring it back with a simple double‑click using the MouseMap profile.

No more guessing. No more resetting display settings.

Profiles 

Built‑In Profiles for Every Workflow

MouseEyes ships with powerful built‑in profiles, including:

  • Two Eyes – Classic dual‑eye cursor tracking

  • Single Eye – Minimal, compact visibility

  • Pointer – Directional pointer instead of eyes

  • MouseMap – Real‑time monitor map with cursor teleport and window recovery

  • CPU & Network – Live system resource monitoring overlay

All profiles can be switched instantly from the right‑click menu.

See Your Mouse. Control Your Desktop.
